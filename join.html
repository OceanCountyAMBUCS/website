Become an Ambuc

Join Us!

Want to make a difference in your community and help improve the lives of people with disabilities? Interested in being a part of the "Fun Way to Serve"? Well, you can do all that and more by becoming a member of AMBUCS!

AMBUCS is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating mobility and independence for people with disabilities. It is a great organization that recruits members from the community to dedicate their time to help others.

AMBUCS believes the joy and freedom of riding a bike is something everyone should be able to experience! Your help is always needed to sponsor individuals on the AmTryke Wish List, or you can make a general donation that will be matched with other donations to fund a tryke!
